Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Everything stays on your iPhone. No servers, no tracking, no data collection.

Introduction

LocBlock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our iOS application LocBlock.

Information We Collect

Short answer: Nothing.

LocBlock is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device:

  1. Location Data: GPS coordinates of your designated "focus locations" - used solely to trigger automatic app blocking when you enter these areas.
  2. App Blocking Preferences: List of apps you choose to block, manual lock preferences, custom location names, and radius settings.
  3. App Usage Data: Break timer history and lock/unlock events - stored securely on-device to power future premium insights.

Data We Do NOT Collect

❌ Your name or email ❌ Location history ❌ App usage patterns ❌ Analytics or tracking ❌ Device identifiers ❌ Social media profiles

How We Use Your Information

All data processing happens locally on your iPhone:

  • Location Data: Used to determine when you enter/exit your designated focus locations and automatically apply app blocking rules.
  • App Preferences: Stored to remember your settings between app launches.
  • Focus History: Stored locally to enable premium insights without ever leaving your iPhone.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored using iOS standard secure storage (UserDefaults and Keychain)
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cloud backup of location data
  • Data is encrypted by iOS when your device is locked
  • We have no access to your data

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  1. Remove Individual Items: Delete locations or clear blocked app selections directly inside LocBlock.
  2. Delete App: Uninstalling LocBlock permanently deletes all stored data.
  3. iOS Settings: Reset Location & Privacy in iOS Settings if needed.

Third-Party Services

LocBlock uses the following Apple frameworks:

  • Apple Family Controls (Screen Time API): Used to enforce app blocking
  • Apple Core Location: Used for geofencing and location-based blocking

These are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. No data is shared with Apple beyond standard iOS functionality.

✓ No analytics services ✓ No advertising networks ✓ No social media SDKs ✓ No cloud storage

Children's Privacy

LocBlock is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store your personal information on our servers, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Remove specific data inside LocBlock or delete the app
  • Portability: Data is stored in standard iOS formats
  • Control: You decide what locations and apps to track

Legal Compliance

LocBlock complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - No personal data collected
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - No personal data sold or shared
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) - Not directed to children under 13
  • Apple App Store Guidelines - Privacy best practices followed

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted:

  1. Within the app (Settings → Privacy Policy)
  2. On our website
  3. In App Store update notes (for material changes)
